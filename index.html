Welcome to Jake's College Kitchen!


Not everyone loves to cook. If you’re a busy college student, it can become a struggle to eat consistent meals every day. I knew this struggle all too well, but once quarantine began and life slowed down, my boyfriend and I took the time to explore cooking. After trying a few recipes, we learned that cooking wasn’t so hard with a little patience and discipline. Now, I’m sharing all of our favorite college meals with you. They’re all simple, quick, affordable and delicious!
