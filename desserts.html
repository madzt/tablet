Desserts Recipes


Fuel your sweet-tooth with these amazing sweet treats, perfect after any meal.

M&M Pretzel Bites

Evenly space pretzels on a baking sheet. Place Hershey Kiss atop each pretzel. Bake in oven at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 10 minutes. Immediately after taking them out of the oven, press an M&M into the center of each Hershey Kiss. Let cool in the refrigerator for 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Hershey Kiss's
  • 10 oz M&M's
  • Pretzels

Apple Pie

Heat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit. Place 1 pie crust in ungreased glass pie pan. Next, in a large mixing bowl, stir flour, sugar, cinnamon, salt, and nutmeg. Add in apples and coat thoroughly in the mixture. Pour mixture on top of the pie crust in the glass pan. Place the top pie crust on top of the pan and press firmly into the sides to close the seal between pie crusts. Bake in the oven for 40 to 45 minutes.

Ingredients


  • 1 box Pillsbury pie crust
  • 6 cups thinly sliced, peeled apples
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg

Oreo Cheesecake

Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Divide 16 Oreos and scoop out filling. Place fillings in a bowl and place cookies in a sealed plastic bag. Crush cookies with a rolling pin or mallet. Put the butter in a bowl and melt it in a microwave, then add the crushed oreo pieces and mix until fully combined. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9‚Äù springform pan. In a large bowl, beat together cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla with an electric mixer. Melt oreo filling in a microwave and stir into the cream cheese mixture. Add eggs and beat lightly to combine them. Chop or crush the 8 remaining oreos. Stir half of the oreos to the cream cheese mixture and pour into the springform pan. Sprinkle the rest of the crushed oreos on top of the cheesecake. Bake for 45 minutes at 350 degrees Fahrenheit, then cool in the refrigerator for at least three hours.

Ingredients


  • 24 OREO cookies
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 3 packages of cream cheese
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 3 eggs
